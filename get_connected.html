LIFEGROUP

The BCM's Lifegroups are student-led weekly Bible studies that meet throughout the week. In these small group studies, we seek to build up one another and equip our students to be disciples who make disciples. Whether studying the Bible or grabbing lunch during the week, Lifegroups are a great place to build relationships and grow closer with peers.

Get Plugged-In

TNT (THURSDAY NIGHT TOGETHER)

THURSDAYS 7:00-8:30

TNT, or Thursday Nights Together, is a weekly night of fellowship for students to grow closer to the LORD as they grow closer with one another. TNT provides the unique opportunity to worship through song, prayer, group discussion, and the sharing of God's Word in the company of other college students as we go through this often hectic stage of life together.

SOUL FOOD

MONDAYS 11:30-1:00

We are honored to have churches in the community serve students by providing a free lunch on Mondays. Soul Food is a time of fellowship and community meant to provide a place of rest as the busy week begins. It is also a great time to connect with the local churches who volunteer their time and resources to minister to us.
