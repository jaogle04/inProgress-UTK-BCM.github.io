Missions

At the BCM, we want to provide students with many opportunities to practically live out Matthew 28. We strive to share the gospel on UT's campus through The Great Exchange, by participating in Disaster Relief projects in the Southeast region, and partnering with the TBMB and IMB to send students on state and international mission trips.
