Fall Retreat

Fall Retreat is a great time to build new friendships and take a small break from classes. The primary focus of this retreat is fellowship and discipleship, with small groups taught by upperclassmen and focusing on a variety of topics.

Barn Party

Dust off your cowboy boots for a night of square dancing, fellowship, and fun! Barn Party is an annual fall event in which we line dance a Friday night away and learn a little more about each other. No dance experience is necessary, only the desire to have some fun! (And maybe brush up on the Cupid Shuffle!)

Winter Retreat

Winter Retreat is a great time to step out of the fall semester's comfort zone and meet someone new. Each year, we spend the weekend diving into a new theme through small groups taught by underclassmen. The goal of this retreat is to build greater connection between our members and provide respite from an often hectic spring semester.

Spring Fling

Spring Fling is our annual banquet and year's end celebration in which we honor the contributions of our graduating seniors, leadership team, and BCM members. Join us in sharing a meal and social time before the summer and final exams send us in different directions!
