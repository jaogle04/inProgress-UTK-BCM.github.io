Our Jerusalem

We strive to share the gospel and reach out to our campus through our weekly coffee cart and spiritual interest surveys. Our coffee cart is out on Ped Walkway weekly offering cool drinks in the warm months and warm drinks in the colder months to anyone who wants a refreshment. In partnership with The Great Exchange, BCM members will have one-on-one conversations with other students on campus through a spiritual interest survey in hopes of leading to a gospel conversation and follow up conversations.

Our Judea

The UTK BCM is proud to partner with the Tennessee Baptist Mission Board (TMBM), through the Disaster Relief and Send TN programs. We annually take a group of students to join a Disaster Relief project in the Southeast where we are able to both meet the needs of a community and grow in fellowship with the more experienced volunteers. Through the Send TN program, indiviual students are able to participate in different aspects of ministry over the summer on assignment throughout the state of Tennessee.

Our Samaria

Many of our missions opportunities push students out of their comfort zones. Each spring break, the UTK BCM travels alongside many other campus ministries from across the country to Panama City Beach as participants in Beach Reach - an effort to provide safe rides and share the Gospel with collegiate students who are vacationing at Panama City Beach. Through North American Mission Board's (NAMB) Gensend Program, individual students are sent on summer assignments throughout the U.S. to serve in and broaden their perspectives of ministry and its many opportunities

The Ends of the Earth

At the UTK BCM, we are honored to facilitate and encourage our students to take part in international mission trips. From Easter Europe to Southeast Asia, the UTK BCM has traveled to various countries in partnership with missionaries from International Mission Board (IMB). Additionally, many of our individual students participate in international summer missions through the IMB, seeing the Gospel go forth across the world.
