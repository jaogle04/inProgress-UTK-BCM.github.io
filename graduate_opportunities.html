Are you looking for an opportunity to grow personally, spiritually, and relationally? Curious to know what daily ministry looks like? Looking for some practical ministry experience before you make a long term ministry decision? If so, the University of Tennessee Knoxville BCM Graduate Assistant Position might be for you!

This ministry opportunity is designed to provide you the opportunity to learn from and minister alongside seasoned campus ministers. It will expose you to all areas of ministry: building into the lives of students, leading small groups, working with international students, planning ministry events, and more. The UTK BCM has been a thriving ministry since 1937. BCM serves UT through weekly evangelism engagement and the community through our SERVE week. Life group Bible studies are active and consistently growing. Our weekly worship on Thursday nights, TNT, reaches around 125 students. A financial stipend can be discussed after further consultation with the BCM.

Minimum Requirement: Ability to commit ten months, bachelor's degree, and some experince in Baptist Collegiate Ministry. Preferred experince: Time spent serving in missions, local and abroad. Experience teaching Bible studies and leading discipleship groups.
