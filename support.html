Support

We are grateful to Tennessee Baptist Churches giving faithfully through the Cooperative Program and the Golden Offering for Tennessee Missions. These gifts fund our campus ministers and the BCM student center, both of which are foundational to our ministry. However, additional gifts from local churches, the Chilhowee, Clinton, and Knox Baptist Association, parents, alumni, and students have graciously funded additional ministry on our campus. Thank you for your continued support and prayers

By giving to the UTK BCM you are making it possible for students to find community, be discipled and ministered to on UT's campus as well as becoming involved in the local church. If you feel led to give to the UTK BCM, the best ways to do so are listed below:

1. Give to the BCM Ministry

Your gifts will be used to cover BCM ministry expenses such as retreats, Bible studies, special speakers and programs, as well as resources and materials used for evangelism, leadership training and equipping students.

2. Give to the BCM Center

Your gifts will be used to cover expenses related to creating community and a home away from home for students. They would cover cost for renovations and improvements to the BCM Center like furniture, sports equipment and technology.

3. Give to the BCM Foundation of Missions

Your gifts will be invested in an endowment fund where we are able to sponsor students to go on mission trips and projects both now and in the future.
If you feel led to volunteer your time to the UTK BCM, the ways to do so are listed below:

  • Fall/Spring Cleaning - windows, dusting, kitchen stove, cabinets, organizig
  • Monday Meals - provide food for Soul Food (weekly from 11:30 - 1:00)
  • Mission Trips - provide funds for a student to participate for international students on UT's campus
  • Building Maintenance - check on building, change filters,light bulbs, and alert the BCM staff to potential repairs or hazards. (time commitment would be approximately one half day per month)
  • Lawn Service - mow the lawn once a week and taek care of maintaining the bushes, trimming, leave raking and the general appearance of the outside of the BCM.
  • Mission Trip Drivers - if you love students, have a good driving record and would like to volunteer to take them on mission trips or even weekends retreats please join the BCM drivers list. (must be over 25)
